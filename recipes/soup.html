Soup

I'm guessing you want to know how to make Soup?

It's very simple actually. Be prepared to make some easy Soup.

Ingredient List

  • 2 cups of cooked Rice
  • 1 Tbsp of Soy Sauce
  • 1 Cup ofDiced Cooked Chicken
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 1/2 cup of Chopped Green Onion

Instructions

  1. Add oil to a pan
  2. Crack the egg then scramble
  3. Add your cooked chicken
  4. Add the rice
  5. Add soy sauce
  6. Add green onion
  7. Serve into two bowls
